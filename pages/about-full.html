About PCVF

Understanding our mission, vision, and commitment to the Philippines

Our Vision

A sovereign Philippines with effective oversight and coordination of visiting forces activities that promote national security, regional stability, and international cooperation while protecting the rights and welfare of Filipino citizens.

Our Mission

To ensure the effective implementation of Status of Forces Agreements (SOFAs) through comprehensive oversight, inter-agency coordination, community engagement, and transparent governance that upholds Philippine sovereignty while fostering beneficial international partnerships.

Core Values

Sovereignty

Protecting and upholding Philippine sovereignty in all international agreements and activities.

Integrity

Maintaining the highest standards of ethical conduct and transparency in all our operations.

Service

Dedicated service to the Filipino people and commitment to public welfare.

Accountability

Ensuring responsible governance and accountability in all decision-making processes.

Legal Basis

Executive Order No. 199

Establishing the Presidential Commission on Visiting Forces and defining its mandate and structure.

Executive Order No. 67

Reorganizing and strengthening the PCVF to enhance oversight capabilities.

Executive Order No. 9

Implementing rules and regulations for SOFA oversight and monitoring.

Executive Order No. 175

Establishing guidelines for community engagement and transparency measures.

Executive Order No. 97

Modernizing PCVF operations and enhancing inter-agency coordination mechanisms.

Organizational Structure

PCVF En Banc

Policy-making body

Secretariat

Administrative and operational support for PCVF activities and coordination.

Oversight Division

Monitoring and evaluation of SOFA implementation and compliance.

Community Relations

Engagement with local communities and stakeholder consultation.

Policy & Research

Policy analysis, research, and advisory services on SOFA matters.

History & Evolution

1999

Establishment of PCVF

Created following the ratification of the PH-US Visiting Forces Agreement to provide oversight and coordination.

2007

Organizational Strengthening

Expansion of PCVF mandate to include enhanced community engagement and transparency measures.

2012

Expanded Coverage

Extension of oversight to include new agreements with Australia and preparations for future agreements.

2020

Digital Transformation

Implementation of digital systems for better monitoring, reporting, and public transparency.

2024

Modern PCVF

Continued evolution with enhanced capabilities for multi-lateral agreement oversight and community partnership.
