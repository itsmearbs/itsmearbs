About PCVF

The Presidential Commission on Visiting Forces (PCVF) is the primary government body responsible for overseeing the implementation of Status of Forces Agreements (SOFAs) in the Philippines.

Established to ensure effective coordination among government agencies and to protect Philippine sovereignty while fostering international cooperation, PCVF plays a crucial role in managing the presence and activities of visiting foreign forces in the country.

Sovereignty Protection

Ensuring Philippine sovereignty while facilitating international cooperation

Partnership Building

Strengthening bilateral and multilateral defense partnerships
